Jayde

Jayde brings energy and charisma as the band’s newest member. Their stage presence is energetic and fun in every performance. Offstage, Jayde’s creativity shines through in their artistic talents, from drawing to doodling. They’re the spark that keeps the band inspired and energized.

Lance

Lance is the creative mastermind behind the band’s music. From writing songs to mixing tracks, he’s the first step that gets the process rolling. Beyond his skillful guitar playing, Lance has been dedicated to booking nearly all the band’s shows and building its fan base. As the first member and founder, Lance is the band’s base, driving its growth and vision since day one.

Lincoln

Lincoln holds together the songs with his basslines, creating dynamic rhythms and melodies. He is passionate in crafting and playing bass lines, providing the foundation for the band’s sound. With three years of experience, Lincoln became the second member of the band after Lance encouraged him to pick up the bass—a decision that created the band. Now, he’s a driving force in the rhythm section.

Luke

Luke is the pulse of the band, creating the energy and c unity in the songs. His always positive attitude keeps spirits high, no matter the circumstances, making him an invaluable member both on and off stage. Luke’s talent and positivity have helped shape the band into what it is today.
